Portrait Photography By Photographer Elizaveta Porodina

New concept of Dark Romanticism

2019-02-18
Art

Elizaveta Porodina is a Russian-Born fashion photographer based in Munich.

Her photography style is varied, among which her use of color is very outstanding, strong and full tone, delicate scenes and modeling,and the light and composition are as beautiful and meaningful as historical paintings. ​

To see more of Elizaveta’s work see here www.porodina.net or follow her at www.instagram.com/elizavetaporodina/
