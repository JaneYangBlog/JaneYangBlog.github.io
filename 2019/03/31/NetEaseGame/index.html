NetEase Game AI Laboratory sharing:AI technology applied in games

2019-03-31

At NetEase Game Developers Summit 2019, Weldon Zhang, Technical Director of Netease Game Artificial Intelligence Lab, shared the exploration and achievements of the artificial intelligence laboratory on the game AI, and performed an inventory of the AI technology applied in the game.

Overall, the five directions that AI technology can use in games are: image API, model animation, voice text, game robot and marketing operations.

The following is a sharing record:

As we all know, China is the largest game market in the world. One is due to the huge number of players, and the other is that it is inseparable from the technological breakthroughs of various companies.

As part of this, Netease also pays great attention to technology accumulation. At the same time, in the seemingly prosperous game market, we also face many challenges. After years of training, players have higher and higher requirements for the quality of the game, and the development cost of the game has increased year by year. At the same time, due to the player’s structural adjustment and market changes, it has hindered our market growth. These problems require us to continue to innovate in technology. AI Lab hopes to serve the game development department through artificial intelligence technology, and hopes to create a boutique game.

First, briefly review the background of the AI game. The AI game appeared with video games. In the early games of “Pacman”, there was a confrontational AI character, and later more complicated NPC AI appeared. Since DeepMind published the DQN article in Nature, the AI game has begun to attract attention. In just three or four years, DQN has developed many variants that solve many functional problems that could not be solved in the past.

For those outside the game industry, the real understanding of AI game may start from AlphaGo, which makes people feel that the spring of AI game is coming. Last year OpenAI Five was able to beat average players in Dota 2.

All of the AI technologies – voice, vision, machine learning – can be applied to games, in addition to the usual AI game .This time, I want to discuss which technologies can be applied in the game, so the starting point is from the technical aspect, not from the product aspect. Next, it is divided into five directions for sharing: image API, model animation, voice text, game robot and marketing operation.
